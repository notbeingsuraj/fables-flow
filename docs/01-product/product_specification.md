# Fables Flow --- Product Specification

## 1. Product Vision & Scope

Fables Flow is an AI-native Order-to-Collection operating system tailored specifically for Indian wholesalers and distributors. The system eliminates manual coordination overhead, stock discrepancies, cash collection delays, and paper invoicing inefficiencies by converting informal client communications (WhatsApp texts, voice inputs, ledger snapshots) into automated, transaction-safe operational workflows.

## 2. Target Personas & Journeys

### Wholesaler/Distributor Owner (Owner)

- **Pain Points**: Lack of visibility into outstanding payments, sales rep productivity, and stock leaks.
- **Key Flow**: Views real-time business health metrics, defines credit policies, overrides credit block thresholds, reviews profitability margins, and manages SaaS billing tiers.

### Accountant (Finance Team)

- **Pain Points**: Reconciling manual payment entries, tracing matching bills, and managing manual GST compliance logs.
- **Key Flow**: Audits incoming digital collection events, resolves outstanding disputes, adjusts Ledger accounts, generates GST reports, and initiates credit notes for returns.

### Sales Representative (Field Agent)

- **Pain Points**: Tracking inventory stock in real-time, verifying customer credit, capturing orders accurately during field visits, and logging manual reports.
- **Key Flow**: Opens the mobile-friendly web view, checks assigned retail routes, verifies a retailer's credit status, places order drafts, and marks payments as physically collected.

### Warehouse Operator (Logistics Team)

- **Pain Points**: Picking errors, lack of location awareness, and tracking batch expiry dates.
- **Key Flow**: Receives picker task allocations, checks rack coordinates, confirms packed items, prints barcode labels, and logs goods received notes (GRN).

### Retailer (Customer Kirana Store Owner)

- **Pain Points**: Needs to call distributors repeatedly to verify invoice totals or place orders.
- **Key Flow**: Communicates via standard WhatsApp Business API. Sends order lists in local dialects or Hinglish, receives digital invoices, views payment link triggers, and checks order dispatch progress.
